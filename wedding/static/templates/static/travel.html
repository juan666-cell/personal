Travel Information

Flights & Airports

The closest airport is Bradley International Airport, which serves the greater Hartford area. The airport code is BDL.

If you are coming in for the rehearsal and rehearsal dinner, you will need to be at Holy Cross Church by 7:00 PM on Friday evening; therefore, please plan your flight to arrive several hours in advance of that time.

Our experience has been that coming from Texas, American Airlines or Delta are usually the least expensive carriers; however, if traveling from other locations on the east coast, Southwest or US Airways are usually the least expensive carriers.

It is also entirely feasible to fly into New York City (JFK) and take the train from there to New Britain; some of our friends have expressed a desire to do this for their own vacationing reasons. If going this route, JetBlue flies nonstop to JFK from most locations. From there, you'll want to take Amtrak from New York City's Penn Station (NYP) to Berlin, CT (BER).

Lodging

We have reserved a block of rooms at the Courtyard Marriott hotel in Farmington, CT, at the discounted rate of $99/night. Call (860) 521-7100 and mention the McClellan/Sneeringer wedding.

If you are coming from out of town and will need a hotel, please consider including yourself in the block if possible, as we have an obligation to fill rooms.

Driving Directions

Bradley International Airport to the Courtyard Marriott

  • Head south from the airport on Schoephoester Rd. for under 1 mile.
  • Schoephoester will turn into CT-401, going south. Continue for 4 miles.
  • Turn right onto I-91 (toward Hartford), going south, for 10 miles.
  • Turn right onto I-84 (toward Waterbury), going west, for 7 miles.
  • Take exit 39A onto CT-9, going south toward New Britain, for 1.5 miles.
  • Take exit 30 onto CT-71 (Southeast Rd.); the hotel will be on the right.

Courtyard Marriott to Holy Cross Church

  • Head south on CT-71 (Southeast Rd.) for just under 2 miles.
  • Turn right on Eddy Glover Blvd.; go just over 1 mile.
  • Turn left onto Farmington Ave. and go a trivial distance.
  • Take the first right onto Biruta St.; Holy Cross Church will be on the right.
