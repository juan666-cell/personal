About Luke

by Meagan

"Blessed is the man who walks not in the counsel of the wicked." (Psalm 1:1)

Luke is a native Texan, born and raised in Austin. In spite of this, he didn’t own a pair of cowboy boots until he met Meagan. (This, perhaps, indicates a crucial difference between Austinites and other Texans.)

True to his Texas blood, he graduated from Texas A&M University and went on to work with a series of thoroughly Texan companies, including H-E-B (a Texas grocery chain) and then several programming firms in Austin, currently a startup named FeedMagnet. All of his jobs incorporated his skill at organization and resource management. They also played to his strength in all things “techy”. Meagan is really marrying him so that she’ll have life-long tech support.

In addition to loving computers (hardware and software, although he’s a Mac loyalist), Luke has a passion for logic and theology. Although these two interests sound like they would be mutually exclusive, when Luke explains it they are inextricably aligned.

In his day-to-day life, Luke wields logic like a scythe to cut through the tangles and snares presented by the modern world. He is also incredibly kind, gentle and genuine, with unmatched problem-solving abilities. These traits combine to make the perfect life partner.
